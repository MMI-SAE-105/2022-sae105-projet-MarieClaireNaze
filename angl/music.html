Grow up by Stray kids: An ode to hope

MaNa thoughts:

I always loved music, even more when I was human. It is very handy to give hope, especially in troubled times.

A music by young...

...to young

Stray kids is a South Korean boyband created in 2017 by the agency JYP entertainment. This group of 8 boys (originally 9) in their early twenties is the source of many famous songs around the world. Since its inception, the group has released numerous EPs and 13 albums aimed at teenagers and young adults that deal with important themes such as self-confidence, coming of age, love, self-perception and many other topics.

The boys-band straykids

Grow up need time

Grow Up is a song from the band's first album "I am not", as the name indicates the lyrics are about coming of age. The text is an encouragement to all teenagers and young adults who are anxious about failing and not succeeding like others. It encourages us to do things at our own pace, as he says in the chorus "you gotta take your time".

"You gotta take your time"

This is the most important sentence of the song, as the tortoise said to the hare "there is no point in running, you have to start on time", in fact we tend to compare ourselves to others to know if we have succeeded in our life, this is the great regret of our generation. ̈But if each one of us would simply follow his own rhythm, life would be easier.

To sum up, Grow up by stray kids is a very good song allowing you to regain self-confidence in front of life, to listen without moderation !
